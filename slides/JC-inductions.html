Journal Club Inductions

General Instructions

  1. Your submitted work is not expected to be a piece of art. Just stay in your comfort zone and make an entry; we praise enthusiasm more than perfection among freshers.
  2. Plagiarism will not be entertained.

Task 1

  • Write 400-500 words about any of the given topics.
  • There are 10 topics in total, and they cover a number of categories like sports, international politics and domestic issues.

Click the down arrow to view the topics.

#1: Liz Truss recently beat Rishi Sunak in the race for the UK prime ministership. What do you think her political positions(on matters like the energy crisis, foreign relations, cost of living crisis etc.) mean for the UK and the world?

#2: Finnish Prime Minister Sanna Marin faced severe backlash after a video of her partying in her own private home went viral. Write an article commenting on this sexist judgement of the situation.

(3) The recently concluded Asia cup tournament served as a huge disappointment for the Indian cricket fans, as the Indian team failed to qualify for the finals. Write a comprehensive piece, analysing the possible reasons/mistakes owing up to the failure and whether this puts India's T20 world cup dreams in jeopardy.

(4) India is going to host the G20 summit this year. Write explaining how will this affect India and the world amidst the global recession and the global tensions.

(5) Mikhail Gorbachev passed away on 30th August 2022. Write about his legacy as the last president of the Soviet Union and his role in ending the cold war

(6) Why does banglore choke everytime it rains? Emphasise on the ways to stop this cycle and all the attempts ever made, if any.

(7) The global pandemic has forced millions globally to work-from-home (WFH) and so is the case in India. Industries such construction, manufacturing, and hospitality faced a severe hit, whereas major tech giants were seen continuing work-from-home for employees even deep into 2022. Write about how the different industries managed to pull through with wfh and the business developments it brought about.

(8) This transfer window Premier league has spent much more than other leagues (€1.35 Billion,as compared to €51.5 million for La Liga,which is second highest in Europe). What might be the reason for this generous spend for premier league? One reason is expansion/promotion of league in Asian countries using flexible timings which allows Asian fans to watch it, while La Liga and other leagues are rigid with their timings, which affects revenue. What are your views on this and the future of these leagues?

(9) Recently Biden nominated an Indian American judge for the New York district court. Before this, the case of Rishi sunak and Kamala Harris were similar wherein people used the Indian identity card abroad to benefit themselves in various ways. Emphasize on this gullible mentality of Indians.

(10) J&K Governor Manoj Sinha states ‘Propaganda about no freedom of speech in India is not true’. Can it be said that freedom of speech in India is curbed under the pressure of adhering to certain ideologies? Comment on the manipulation of legislation affecting true free speech (You can also comment on the recent controversies involving arrests of comedians and public speakers and labelling people anti-national over petty ideological differences).

Task 2

Write a short report evaluating, criticizing or commenting (sarcastically) on any event, occasion, problem, nuisance or reality of campus or inventing an imaginary event on campus that relates to a what-if situation. (200-250 words)

Towards the end, write a paragraph on why you wish to join the Journal Club and what you expect from the club and as a part of the student community, what would you like to read about in our articles?
